# Introduction

This website is created for all Murakami lovers who have always longed for a curated collection of every pop culture reference spanning his awe-inspiring novels.

Whether you are a devoted Murakami fan seeking to go deep into his works or a curious wanderer eager to explore his most famous works, this website will hopefully be your gateway to a comprehensive list of all references you have found it hard to keep track of.

# How it works

All current Murakami works are grouped within 3 categories: `Novels`, `Short Story Collections` and `Non-Fiction`. Click on the name of any book listed to see a full list of pop culture references within the book.

Otherwise, you can simply search _anything_ on the top right search bar, not only by book title but directly by the reference you are looking for.
